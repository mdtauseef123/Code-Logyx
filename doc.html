1.Prime Numbers

One of the most common question which is being asked in an every SDE interiew is to check whether a number is Prime or not.There are so many ways the question can be asked so lets's kick-start with a simple question What is a Prime Number?A number is said to be prime if it has exactly two factors one and itself.For example,3 is a Prime Number because it only has two factors i.e. 1 and 3 itself.Similarly 11 is also a Prime as it has two factors i.e. 1 and 11 itself.
Note:- 1 is also a Prime Number as 1 is divided by 1 and 1 itself.

Now there are so many ways to check whether a number is prime or not

i.Brute Force Approach:-To check every factor and count the number of factor.If the number of factor is 2 then it is prime number otherwise it is not a prime number.
